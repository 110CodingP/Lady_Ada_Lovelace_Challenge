## Lady Ada Lovelace Challenge
This repository maintains a record of the submissions made as part of learning period of Lady Ada Lovelace Challenge, a hackathon for 1st year students at IIT Kanpur to build impactful tech!

### Theme Selected: Empowering Connections & Professional Growth
To develop sophisticated platforms that address specific academic, professional, and personal growth challenges for women in STEM at IIT Kanpur. These projects should move beyond simple social networks to create tangible value and foster a robust support ecosystem.
